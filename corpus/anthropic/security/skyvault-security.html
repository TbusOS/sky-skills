SkyVault is a self-hosted secrets manager. Every value is encrypted on your machine before it ever reaches the server — so the server, and anyone who breaches it, only ever sees ciphertext. This page is the whole story: the threat model we defend, the cryptography we use, who has audited it, and how to report a hole. SkyVault 是一个自托管的密钥管理器。每个值在你的机器上加密后才会到达服务器 —— 所以服务器（以及任何攻破它的人）永远只看到密文。这一页把话说全：我们防的威胁模型、用的密码学、谁审计过、以及怎么报漏洞。

Threat model威胁模型

What we defend — and what we don't.我们防什么 —— 以及不防什么。

A security page that only lists wins is marketing. Here is the honest boundary.只列优点的安全页是广告。这里是诚实的边界。

Defended防得住

A breached server服务器被攻破

An attacker with full disk and database access on the server reads only ciphertext. No passphrase, no derived key, and no plaintext is ever stored or logged server-side.拿到服务器整盘和数据库的攻击者只能读到密文。密码、派生密钥、明文都不在服务端存储或记录。

Defended防得住

A passive network网络被监听

Traffic is TLS 1.3 on the wire and already-encrypted at the payload layer. A logged proxy or a compromised CDN sees double-wrapped ciphertext, not values.传输层是 TLS 1.3，负载本身已加密。被记录的代理或被攻陷的 CDN 看到的是双层密文，不是明文值。

Out of scope不在范围

Your own endpoint你自己的终端

If malware owns the device where you type the passphrase, it can read plaintext at the source. Client-side encryption cannot defend a compromised client — we are explicit about that rather than implying magic.如果恶意软件控制了你输入密码的设备，它能在源头读到明文。客户端加密防不住被攻陷的客户端 —— 我们把这点说清楚，不假装有魔法。

Found a hole? We want it.发现漏洞？我们想知道。

Email security@skyvault.dev with the PGP key linked from /.well-known/security.txt. We acknowledge within 24 hours, agree a fix timeline within 5 days, and publish an advisory within 90 days — whether or not the fix is ready, because a deadline you can't see isn't a commitment.发邮件到 security@skyvault.dev，PGP 公钥在 /.well-known/security.txt。我们 24 小时内确认收到，5 天内商定修复时间表，90 天内发布公告 —— 无论修复是否完成，因为你看不见的期限不算承诺。

We run a paid bounty, not a hall-of-fame-and-a-handshake. Researchers who report in good faith are never threatened with legal action — that promise is in writing in our safe-harbor policy.我们给真金白银的赏金，不是"名人堂加握手"。善意报告的研究者绝不会被法律威胁 —— 这条承诺白纸黑字写在我们的安全港政策里。

How it works工作方式

A folder, a keyword, a verdict.一个文件夹、一个关键词、一个判决。

01 · A skill一个技能

1 folder文件夹

SKILL.md is the contract, references hold the craft, assets hold the CSS. No registry, no build — delete the folder and it's gone.SKILL.md 是契约，references 放工艺，assets 放 CSS。没有注册表、没有构建——删掉文件夹，它就消失了。

02 · TRIGGER

0 imports手动引入

Every SKILL.md carries TRIGGER keywords. Say "keynote" and eclat loads itself — its tokens, its canon, its gates — with nothing wired by hand.每份 SKILL.md 都写着 TRIGGER 关键词。你说出"发布会"，eclat 就自己加载——它的 token、范本、检查——一行都不用手动接。

03 · The verdict判决

4 gates道检查

Generators ship, design-review judges — structure, rendered audit, screenshot, then an LLM critic. Fail any gate and the page does not ship.生成器负责生成，design-review 负责评判——结构、渲染审计、截图，再加一位 LLM 评审。任何一道不过，页面就发不出去。

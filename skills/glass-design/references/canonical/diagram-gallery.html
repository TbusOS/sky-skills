04 · Diagram craft图示工艺

Why these read
as one system. 为什么它们读起来
是同一个系统。

None of these diagrams import a style. They reuse the page's own material — node fills are panel glass, lines are the page's hairlines, and cyan means exactly one thing: where the energy goes. 这些图没有引入任何外来风格。它们复用页面自己的材质——节点就是面板玻璃，线就是页面的发丝线，而 cyan 永远只表达一件事：能量去哪里。

R·1

Same material as the page与页面同材质

Node fill is the panel token, stroke is the border token. A diagram pasted from elsewhere reads as a sticker on glass. 节点填充用面板 token，描边用边框 token。从别处贴来的图，在玻璃上读起来就是一张贴纸。

R·2

Cyan is a verb, not a paintcyan 是动词，不是涂料

At most three cyan elements per diagram: the flow, the focus, the live marker. Zero cyan is a wireframe; four is a christmas tree. 每张图至多三个 cyan 元素：流向、焦点、live 标记。零个是线稿，四个是圣诞树。

R·3

Theme-proof ink主题免疫的墨

Every label inks through the .glass-svg tokens, so the same SVG survives the light theme untouched. Hardcoded white text vanishes at noon. 每个标签都通过 .glass-svg token 上墨，同一张 SVG 切到 light 主题原样存活。写死的白字到了白天就消失。

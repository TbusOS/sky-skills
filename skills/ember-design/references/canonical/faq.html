Pricing & billing定价与账单

What it costs. What it doesn't. 要花的钱，和不用花的钱。

Our whole price list is three lines — Free, $9, $29. These five answers cover everything that lives around those lines. 我们的价目表一共三行 —— 免费、$9、$29。下面五个回答，把这三行周围的事都讲清楚。

Is Free really free forever, or a trial wearing a coat?免费版真是永久免费，还是披着外套的试用?+−

Free forever, and we mean the words separately: free, and forever. The Free plan is the whole editor — unlimited notes, full Markdown + MDX, export to PDF, Epub and Markdown, 50 local backlinks — running entirely on your machine, no account required. What Pro charges for is encrypted sync between devices. If you write on one machine, you may never owe us anything, and that's fine with us. 永久免费，而且这两个词分开都成立：免费，并且永久。免费版就是完整的编辑器 —— 无限笔记、完整 Markdown + MDX、导出 PDF/Epub/Markdown、50 条本地反向链接 —— 全部在你本机运行，无需注册。Pro 收费的只有跨设备加密同步。如果你只在一台机器上写作，可能永远不用付我们一分钱，我们觉得这样很好。

What exactly does the $9 on Pro buy?Pro 的 $9 到底买到什么?+−

Encrypted cloud sync across 5 devices, unlimited backlinks and the graph view, 90 days of version history, the iOS and Android apps, and publishing any note to name.skypad.page. Pro is $9/month billed yearly, and the 14-day trial asks for no card — we'd rather you leave easily than stay by accident. 跨 5 台设备的加密云同步、无限反向链接和关系图、90 天版本历史、iOS 和 Android 应用，以及把任意笔记发布到 name.skypad.page。Pro $9/月、按年付费；14 天试用不要信用卡 —— 我们宁可你轻松离开，也不想你因为忘了取消而留下。

Do I lose my notes if I cancel Pro?取消 Pro 后会丢笔记吗?+−

No. Every note is a plain Markdown file on your own disk — cancelling changes nothing about the files themselves. Sync stops, version history trims from 90 days to 7, and the mobile apps go read-only. Resubscribe later and sync picks up exactly where it left off. 不会。每条笔记都是你磁盘上的普通 Markdown 文件 —— 取消订阅不改变文件本身的任何东西。同步停止，版本历史从 90 天缩到 7 天，移动端变成只读。以后重新订阅，同步会从停下的那一刻继续。

Is there a student or low-income price?有学生价或低收入折扣吗?+−

Pro is $3/month if you send a photo of your student ID, or anything showing you're on a public-benefit program. No form, no review committee, no annual re-verification — people who need the discount shouldn't have to prove it twice. Pro 是 $3/月 —— 发一张学生证照片，或任何能说明你在领公共福利的材料就行。没有申请表，没有评审委员会，不用每年重新证明 —— 需要折扣的人不该被要求证明两次。

How does Team billing handle someone joining mid-month?Team 账单怎么处理月中加入的成员?+−

Seats are prorated by the day: a writer joining on the 20th costs a third of that month. Removed seats stay active until the billing cycle ends, then quietly leave the invoice. And before you buy anything, the Team plan has a 30-day pilot for up to 5 seats, no credit card — email team@skypad.page and we'll set it up within one business day. 席位按天折算：20 号加入的人，当月只收三分之一。移除的席位用到当期账单周期结束，然后从发票上安静消失。而且在花钱之前，Team 有 30 天试点，最多 5 个席位，不要信用卡 —— 发邮件到 team@skypad.page，一个工作日内开通。

Notes, sync & devices笔记、同步与设备

Where your words live. 你的文字住在哪里。

"Your notes are plain files on your disk" is a claim better shown than said — so here it is, shown first. 「你的笔记是磁盘上的普通文件」这句话，与其说出来，不如画出来 —— 所以先画给你看。

Where do my notes actually live?我的笔记到底存在哪里?+−

In a folder of .md files on your disk — ~/Skypad by default, or any folder you point it at, including one inside Dropbox or iCloud Drive. There is no proprietary database, which is exactly what the picture above shows. Open the folder in VS Code or Obsidian and everything is simply there. 在你磁盘上的一个 .md 文件夹里 —— 默认是 ~/Skypad，也可以指到任何文件夹，包括 Dropbox 或 iCloud Drive 里的。没有私有数据库，上面那张图画的就是这件事。用 VS Code 或 Obsidian 打开那个文件夹，所有东西都原样在那里。

What happens if two devices edit the same note offline?两台设备离线时改了同一条笔记，会怎样?+−

We keep both versions. The later sync writes a sibling file — on-morning-walks (conflict 2026-06-08).md — next to the original, and the app shows the two side by side so you merge by reading, not by trusting an algorithm. Skypad never silently merges or discards an edit. 两个版本都保留。后同步的那台会在原文件旁边写一个并存文件 —— on-morning-walks (conflict 2026-06-08).md —— 应用把两版并排展示，你读着合并，而不是把判断交给某个算法。Skypad 从不悄悄合并或丢弃任何修改。

Does Skypad work fully offline?Skypad 能完全离线用吗?+−

Completely. Free never opens a network connection at all. On Pro, edits queue locally and sync when you're back online — a long flight or a cabin week changes nothing about how the editor behaves. Offline isn't a degraded mode; it's the native one. 完全可以。免费版根本不会发起网络连接。Pro 上，修改先在本地排队，联网后再同步 —— 一趟长途航班或一周山里小屋，编辑器的行为不会有任何变化。离线不是降级模式，而是本来的模式。

What counts as one of my 5 Pro devices?Pro 的 5 台设备是怎么算的?+−

Any install signed into your vault: Mac, Windows, Linux, iPhone, Android — each is one. Deauthorize an old device any time from Settings → Devices and the slot frees immediately. Published name.skypad.page pages don't count; readers aren't devices. 登录了你保险库的任何安装实例都算一台：Mac、Windows、Linux、iPhone、Android，各算一个。随时可以在「设置 → 设备」里注销旧设备，名额立刻释放。发布出去的 name.skypad.page 页面不占名额 —— 读者不是设备。

Security & privacy安全与隐私

What we can't see, and why. 我们看不到什么，为什么。

"End-to-end encrypted" is the claim you have the most right to doubt — so it comes as a picture first, then as five answers. 「端到端加密」是你最有权怀疑的说法 —— 所以先给你一张图，再给五个回答。

What does "end-to-end encrypted" actually mean here?这里说的「端到端加密」具体指什么?+−

Exactly what the picture above shows. Your note is encrypted on your device with XChaCha20-Poly1305, using a key derived from your passphrase — before a single byte leaves the machine. Skypad's servers store and forward ciphertext; the key never travels. Your other device derives the same key from the same passphrase and decrypts locally. 就是上图画的那样。笔记在你的设备上用 XChaCha20-Poly1305 加密，密钥从你的口令派生 —— 在任何一个字节离开本机之前。Skypad 的服务器只存储和转发密文，密钥从不上路。你的另一台设备用同一口令派生出同一把密钥，在本地解密。

Can the three of you read my notes?你们三个人能读到我的笔记吗?+−

We can't — and "can't" is the load-bearing word, not a promise of good manners. The servers hold ciphertext, encrypted filenames, sizes and timestamps; no keys, no plaintext, no titles. A subpoena gets the same ciphertext the diagram shows. This is also why we can't "just restore" your notes if you lose your passphrase. 读不到 —— 这里的「读不到」是承重墙，不是礼貌性承诺。服务器上只有密文、加密后的文件名、大小和时间戳；没有密钥，没有明文，没有标题。法院传票拿到的也是图里那份密文。这也是为什么口令丢了，我们没办法「帮你恢复」。

What if I forget my passphrase?忘记口令了怎么办?+−

During setup, Skypad prints you a one-time recovery kit — a single page with a 24-word code. That page is the only way back in. We can't reset the passphrase for you, because resetting would require us to hold a key, and the entire design is that we don't. Print the kit. Keep it where your passport lives. 初次设置时，Skypad 会给你打印一份一次性恢复套件 —— 一页纸，上面是 24 个词的代码。那页纸是唯一的回路。我们没法替你重置口令：重置意味着我们得持有密钥，而整个设计就是我们不持有。把它打印出来，放在你放护照的地方。

Who audits Skypad, and how often?谁来审计 Skypad，多久一次?+−

An independent security firm audits the sync protocol and the desktop and mobile clients every 6 months, and the full report — findings, severity, fixes — is published at skypad.page/security. The latest audit closed in March 2026 with zero critical findings. Every report since the first is public, including the awkward ones. 一家独立安全公司每 6 个月审计一次同步协议和桌面、移动客户端，完整报告 —— 发现、级别、修复 —— 发布在 skypad.page/security。最近一次审计于 2026 年 3 月结束，0 个严重项。从第一份起，每份报告都公开，包括不体面的那几份。

Is Skypad GDPR-compliant? Where are the servers?Skypad 符合 GDPR 吗?服务器在哪里?+−

Sync servers run in Frankfurt, Germany, under EU jurisdiction. We're GDPR-compliant the unexciting way: minimal metadata, a signed DPA for Team customers, and deletion that actually deletes — close your account and the ciphertext is purged within 30 days. Team plans can additionally pin data residency to the EU or US region. 同步服务器在德国法兰克福，受欧盟司法管辖。我们用最不刺激的方式符合 GDPR：最少化的元数据、给 Team 客户的签字 DPA，以及真正会删除的删除 —— 注销账号后，密文 30 天内清除。Team 套餐还可以把数据驻留固定在欧盟或美国区。

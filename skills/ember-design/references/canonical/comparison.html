Skypad is local-first. Notion, Evernote, and Google Keep are cloud-native. Each design choice rules out things the other makes easy. Below, we'll show both — honestly — including the cases where Skypad isn't the right call. Skypad 是本地优先。Notion、印象笔记、Google Keep 是云端原生。每一个设计决定都会关掉另一边擅长的事。下面诚实地两边都讲 —— 包括 Skypad 不适合你的场景。

The two truths两种事实

Neither side is a smaller version of the other. 两边都不是对方的缩小版。

Each was designed fully from its first principles. Below, both claims read in their honest voice. 两种笔记都从各自的第一性原理完整设计而来。下面用诚实的口吻分别讲两边的主张。

Local-first · Skypad本地优先 · Skypad

Your notes live on your disk. Sync is optional. 笔记在你的磁盘上。同步是可选项。

Writing is offline by default. Cloud sync is an opt-in switch — and when you turn it on, it's end-to-end encrypted, so Skypad itself can't read your notes. The data format is plain Markdown, openable by any text editor — twenty years from now too. 默认离线写作。云同步是你主动打开的开关 —— 打开后也是端到端加密，Skypad 自己读不到你的内容。数据是普通 Markdown，任何文本编辑器都能打开 —— 二十年后也能。

  • Offline is the default state, not a degraded fallback.离线是默认状态，不是降级模式。
  • Vendor going under = your notes are still on your disk.厂商倒闭 = 笔记还在你的磁盘上。
  • Plain Markdown — grep, git, any editor works on the file.普通 Markdown —— grep、git、任何编辑器都直接能用。
  • No vendor (us included) can read your content.任何厂商（包括我们）都读不到你的内容。
Cloud-native · Notion / Evernote云端原生 · Notion / 印象笔记

Your notes live on a server. Clients are windows into it. 笔记在服务器上。客户端是通向它的窗口。

The vendor server is the source of truth. Every device renders the same live state. Collaboration is native — two people on the same page see each other typing. The trade-off: you trust the vendor, you need an account, and when the vendor is down, so is your notebook. 服务器是唯一真实源。每个设备渲染同一份实时状态。多人协作是原生能力 —— 两人同改一页时互相看到光标。代价：你要信任厂商、需要账号，厂商宕机时你的笔记本也宕机。

  • Real-time collaboration with zero plumbing.多人实时协作，零配置。
  • Rich block types — tables, databases, embeds — beyond plain text.丰富的内容块类型 —— 表格、数据库、嵌入 —— 远超纯文本。
  • State stays the same across all devices, all the time.所有设备状态始终一致。
  • Vendor-backed search, permissions, and admin tooling.厂商提供搜索、权限、管理工具。

Pick the right one选对那一个

Three clear scenarios. 三个清晰的场景。

Skip the long pro-and-con matrix. The real decision usually collapses to one of these three. 跳过长长的优劣表格。真实的选择通常落在这三种里的一种。

Pick Skypad选 Skypad

Solo thinker, long horizon 独立思考者，长时间跨度

You keep a journal, a reading log, or a research vault. You want those notes readable in twenty years, searchable without waiting on anyone's servers, and not tied to a subscription you might one day stop paying. Local-first is the only pattern that survives all three tests at once. 你在记日记、写读书笔记或一份研究素材库。希望这些笔记二十年后还能读到、搜索时不用等服务器、也不被订阅费绑住。本地优先是唯一一次过这三关的模式。

Pick Notion / Evernote选 Notion / 印象

Team editing the same page daily 团队每天共编同一页

Five teammates editing the same roadmap, all at once. Threaded comments, shared permissions, an admin history you can audit. Local-first can imitate this with CRDTs and some plumbing — but cloud-native gives it to you with zero setup. If real-time collaboration is your core workflow, the cloud's native advantage wins. 五个同事同时改一份 roadmap。评论线、共享权限、可追溯的管理历史。本地优先可以靠 CRDT 加配置模拟，但云端原生零配置就能给你。如果实时协作是你的核心工作流，云端原生的优势压倒本地优先。

Either works两者皆可

Short-term, low-stakes 短期项目，低风险

A trip itinerary. A week-long shopping list. A recipe collection cloned off a blog you'll never edit again. Either side serves these fine — pick whichever you have open. Long-term trade-offs don't compound on a workload that won't outlive a fortnight. 一次出行的行程。一周购物清单。从博客复制来再也不改的菜谱合集。两边都够用 —— 哪个已经打开就用哪个。两周内就用完的事，长期权衡撑不到那一天。

An honest closing诚实的结尾
Skypad is local-first. That's a trade-off, not a universal winner. If real-time collaboration is your main workflow, use Notion. If you want notes you'll still be reading in twenty years, use us. The worst tool is the one chosen by following a trend — match the tool to the work, not to the marketing. Skypad 是本地优先。这是一种取舍，不是普适的赢家。如果实时协作是你的主工作流，用 Notion。如果你希望二十年后还能读这些笔记，用我们。最糟糕的工具是跟风选来的那个 —— 让工具匹配你的工作，不是让它匹配营销。

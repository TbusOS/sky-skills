FAQ

Questions worth asking.

Do I lose my notes if I cancel Pro?

No. Every note you ever wrote is a plain Markdown file on your own machine — that's the local-first in local-first. If you cancel, sync stops and version history trims to 7 days, but your notes stay exactly where they were. Export runs the same on Free.

What does "end-to-end encrypted" actually mean?

Your notes are encrypted on your device with a key derived from your passphrase, before they leave your machine. Skypad servers store ciphertext; we can't read your notes, and we can't recover them if you lose your passphrase. We ship an open-source audit report every 6 months.

Is there an AI-assist feature?

No — and that's a product decision, not a roadmap item. The reason people stay on Skypad is that it's the one place that isn't trying to complete their sentences. If you want AI in your editor, Skypad isn't for you, and we're not upset about it.

Can my team try it before committing to 10 seats?

Yes. The Team plan has a 30-day pilot for up to 5 seats, no credit card. Email team@skypad.page with your company and we'll set it up within one business day.

I'm a student or on a low income. Discount?

Pro is $3/month if you send us a photo of your student ID, or any evidence you're on a public-benefit program. No application, no review committee. We don't make the form hard — people who need the discount shouldn't also have to prove it twice.

Changelog · Skypad 3.x更新日志 · Skypad 3.x

Every change,
written down. 每一次改动，
都写下来。

Skypad ships slowly, on purpose. When something changes, we write down what, and why, in plain language — the way you'd explain it to a friend across the table. Skypad 发布得很慢，这是有意的。每次改动，我们都用白话写清楚改了什么、为什么——就像隔着桌子向朋友解释那样。

Release notes更新记录

What changed, month by month. 逐月记下的每一处改动。

Each note is written by whoever made the change. Patches get two lines; releases get a letter. 每条记录由做出改动的人执笔。修订只写两行，正式版本则是一封信。

June 20262026 年 6 月
06 · 04 · 2026 v3.4

The graph, quieter.关系图，安静了。

The backlink graph used to dance every time you opened it. Now it settles, and stays put. 反向链接图以前每次打开都要跳一阵舞。现在它会安定下来，停在原处。

  • Added新增Graph filters — limit the view to one folder or one tag.图谱筛选——只看某个文件夹或某个标签下的链接。
  • Added新增Hover a node to preview the note without leaving the graph.悬停节点即可预览笔记，不用离开图谱。
  • Changed调整Layout settles in under a second on a 2,000-note graph. It used to take four.两千条笔记的图谱，布局一秒内稳定。以前要四秒。
  • Fixed修复Orphan notes no longer drift off-canvas in small windows.孤立笔记不会再在小窗口里漂出画布。
May 20262026 年 5 月
05 · 21 · 2026 v3.3.1

A quiet patch.一个安静的修订。

Two sync bugs, both reported by the same careful reader in Osaka. Thank you, M. 两个同步问题，都来自大阪同一位细心读者的报告。谢谢你，M。

  • Fixed修复Editing the same note offline on two devices no longer duplicates footnotes on merge.两台设备离线编辑同一条笔记，合并时不再复制脚注。
  • Fixed修复The iOS keyboard no longer covers the last line while a sync banner is visible.同步提示出现时，iOS 键盘不再遮住最后一行。
05 · 07 · 2026 v3.3

Publish, with footnotes.发布，带上脚注。

Your published pages now carry everything the note does — footnotes included. 你发布的页面现在能呈现笔记里的一切——包括脚注。

  • Added新增Footnotes render on skypad.page, with return links back to the text.脚注可以在 skypad.page 上渲染，带返回正文的链接。
  • Added新增Two new publishing typefaces, both open-source.两款新的发布字体，均为开源。
  • Changed调整Published pages self-host their fonts — your readers never touch a font CDN.发布页字体改为自托管——你的读者不会再请求字体 CDN。
April 20262026 年 4 月
04 · 09 · 2026 v3.2

Search that reads Markdown.懂 Markdown 的搜索。

We rebuilt search from scratch. It understands headings, code blocks and quotes — and it works on a plane. 我们从头重写了搜索。它能区分标题、代码块和引用——而且在飞机上也能用。

  • Added新增Filters: in:journal, before:2026-05, has:code.筛选语法：in:journal、before:2026-05、has:code。
  • Added新增CJK-aware segmentation — Chinese and Japanese queries match on word boundaries.中日文分词——按词边界正确命中，不再整句匹配。
  • Changed调整The index lives on your disk. Search is fully offline.索引存在你的磁盘上。搜索完全离线。
  • Fixed修复Smart quotes no longer break exact-phrase search.弯引号不再破坏精确短语搜索。
March 20262026 年 3 月
03 · 18 · 2026 v3.1.2

Small fixes from the February letters.来自二月读者来信的小修复。

  • Fixed修复The cursor no longer jumps after pasting more than 10,000 words.粘贴超过一万词后，光标不再跳动。
  • Fixed修复The device list could crash with more than five devices linked.绑定超过五台设备时，设备列表可能崩溃。
  • Changed调整Updates download as deltas — about 8 MB instead of 60.更新改为增量下载——大约 8 MB，而不是 60 MB。
February 20262026 年 2 月
02 · 12 · 2026 v3.1

Five devices, one key each.五台设备，各持一钥。

Encryption you can see and manage — not just take on trust. 加密不再只是「请相信我们」，而是看得见、管得住。

  • Added新增Device manager — every device holds its own key. Revoke any of them in one click.设备管理——每台设备一把独立密钥，可一键吊销。
  • Added新增Yearly key rotation, automatic and silent.密钥每年自动轮换，静默完成。
  • Changed调整The recovery passphrase is now set up before your first sync, not after.恢复口令改为首次同步前设置，而不是之后。
January 20262026 年 1 月
01 · 15 · 2026 v3.0

The rebind.重新装订。

A new editor engine, three years in the making. Everything feels the same — only faster. 全新编辑器引擎，做了三年。一切手感如旧——只是更快了。

  • Added新增New rendering engine — keystroke latency under 8 ms on a 2019 laptop.新渲染引擎——2019 年的笔记本上击键延迟低于 8 ms。
  • Changed调整Themes rebuilt on design tokens. Your old theme migrates automatically.主题改用 design token 重建，旧主题自动迁移。
  • Fixed修复Forty-one long-standing editor bugs — the full list is in the release letter.四十一个陈年编辑器问题——完整清单见随版本信。
Process流程

How a release ships. 一个版本如何发布。

Every version walks the same four weeks. If the beta ring finds a regression, the date moves — the bar doesn't. 每个版本都走同样的四周。如果测试圈发现回归，推迟的是日期，不降的是标准。

I read their changelog even when nothing is broken. It's the only release page I know that reads like a letter, not a press release. 什么都没坏的时候，我也会读他们的更新日志。这是我知道的唯一一个读起来像信、而不像新闻稿的发布页。

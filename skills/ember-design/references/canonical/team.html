How we work我们怎么工作

The day travels west. 一天，向西走。

No standing meetings. Kyoto starts the day, Berlin carries it, Toronto closes it — each city leaves written notes for the next, like a ship's logbook. 没有例会。京都开始这一天，柏林接着，多伦多收尾——每座城市给下一座留下书面交接，像一本航海日志。

House rules工坊的规矩

Three habits, kept. 三个习惯，一直守着。

Slow is a feature.慢，是功能。

One release each season — finished, or not at all. We'd rather ship a quiet October than promise a loud March. 每季一个版本——做完才发，做不完就不发。宁要一个安静的十月，不要一个喧哗的三月的承诺。

Everyone answers mail.每个人都回邮件。

Tuesday mornings, all nine of us read support mail — founders included. Nothing teaches the product like the person it confused. 每周二上午，九个人一起读支持邮件，创始人也不例外。没有什么比被产品困住的那个人，更能教我们做产品。

Handoffs, not meetings.交接，而不是会议。

The logbook note at the end of each city's day is the only ritual we keep. If it can't be written down, it isn't decided yet. 每座城市一天结束时的日志，是我们唯一保留的仪式。写不下来的事，就还没有定。

We don't want Skypad to be big. We want it to be finished — the way a good chair is finished. 我们不想让 Skypad 变大。我们想让它被做完——像一把好椅子那样，被做完。
Mara Lindqvist — co-founder, Berlin Mara Lindqvist——联合创始人，柏林

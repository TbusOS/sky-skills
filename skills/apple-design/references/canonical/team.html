How we work. 我们怎么工作。

A quiet product comes from a quiet way of working. Three rules we've kept since the first commit. 安静的产品来自安静的工作方式。三条规则，从第一次提交守到现在。

Write first, meet once.先写下来，每周只开一次会。

Every decision starts as a Markdown note in the shared vault — argued in comments, decided in writing. The whole company meets for one hour a week, and that's it. 每个决定都从共享笔记库里的一条 Markdown 笔记开始——在批注里争论，以文字定稿。全公司每周只开一小时会，仅此而已。

Small on purpose.小，是有意的。

Eight people means no managers of managers, no handoffs, no roadmap theater. The person who proposed a feature builds it, ships it, and answers for it. 八个人意味着没有层层汇报，没有交接，没有路线图表演。谁提出功能，谁来构建、上线，并对它负责。

Why we won't grow fast为什么不快速扩张

Support is engineering.支持工作就是工程工作。

There is no support department. Tickets route to whoever wrote the code, so bugs hurt the right person — and get fixed in days, not quarters. 没有客服部门。工单直接派给写那段代码的人，让 bug 疼在正确的人身上——几天内修好，而不是几个季度。

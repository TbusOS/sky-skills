Pricing & billing.定价与付费。

Free is free forever. Pro is $9 a month or $90 a year. Team is $29 a seat. Everything below follows from that. 免费版永久免费。Pro 每月 $9 或每年 $90。Team 每席位 $29。下面的一切都由此而来。

If I cancel Pro, do I lose my notes?取消 Pro 后，我的笔记会丢吗？

No. Every note is a plain .md file on your own disk — cancelling stops sync and publishing, nothing else. Your files stay exactly where they are, readable by any editor, forever. 不会。每条笔记都是你磁盘上的纯 .md 文件——取消只是停掉同步和发布，别的什么都不动。文件原地不动，任何编辑器都能打开，永远如此。

Monthly or annual — can I switch later?按月还是按年——以后能切换吗？

Yes, any time, from Settings → Billing. Annual is $90 — two months free against monthly. Switching mid-cycle is prorated to the day; the difference is credited to your next invoice automatically. 可以，随时在「设置 → 付费」切换。按年是 $90——比按月省两个月。周期中途切换按天折算，差额自动抵扣到下一张账单。

What's the refund policy?退款政策是什么？

30 days, no questions asked, on any first purchase of Pro or Team. Email billing@skypad.app from your account address; the refund lands in 3–5 business days. After 30 days we prorate instead of refusing. 首次购买 Pro 或 Team，30 天内退款，不问原因。用账户邮箱发信到 billing@skypad.app，退款 3–5 个工作日到账。超过 30 天我们按比例折算，而不是直接拒绝。

I'm a student or on a low income. Is there a discount?我是学生或收入有限，有折扣吗？

Yes — 50% off Pro. A school email address qualifies automatically; if you don't have one, a one-line self-declaration is enough. We don't ask for documents, and we don't expire the discount mid-degree. 有——Pro 半价。学校邮箱自动通过；没有的话，一句话的自我声明就够了。我们不要证明材料，也不会在你读到一半时取消折扣。

How do Team seats work when people join or leave?成员加入或离开时，Team 席位怎么算？

Add or remove seats any day; billing is prorated to the day on the next invoice. A removed member's personal vault stays on their device — only access to shared workspaces ends. Teams up to 10 seats can trial for 14 days, no card. 任意一天都可以增减席位，下一张账单按天折算。被移除成员的个人笔记库仍留在其设备上——只是失去共享工作空间的访问权。10 席以内的团队可免费试用 14 天，无需信用卡。

Sync, security & your data.同步、安全与数据。

The short version: your notes are encrypted before they leave your device, and the keys never do. 一句话版本：笔记离开你的设备之前已经加密，而密钥永远不离开。

What does "end-to-end encrypted" actually mean?「端到端加密」具体指什么？

Each note is encrypted on your device with AES-256-GCM before sync. The key is derived from your passphrase and never uploaded — see the figure above. Our servers store ciphertext they cannot decrypt; neither can we, a subpoena, or an attacker who steals our database. 每条笔记在同步前就在你的设备上用 AES-256-GCM 加密。密钥由你的口令派生，从不上传——见上图。我们的服务器只存解不开的密文；我们解不开，传票解不开，偷走数据库的攻击者也解不开。

What happens if I lose all my devices?如果我所有设备都丢了怎么办？

Your 24-word recovery phrase — generated at setup, shown once — restores everything on a new device. Without it and without any signed-in device, your notes are unrecoverable, by us included. That's not a gap in our support; it's the design that makes the previous answer true. 初始设置时生成、只显示一次的 24 个单词恢复短语，可以在新设备上恢复全部内容。如果恢复短语和已登录设备都没有了，笔记就无法找回——我们也不行。这不是支持的漏洞，正是让上一条回答成立的设计。

Where are the servers, and can I choose the region?服务器在哪里？可以选区域吗？

Frankfurt (EU) and Oregon (US). You pick a region at sign-up and can migrate later from Settings — migration re-uploads ciphertext, so the move changes nothing about who can read your notes. Team plans include a GDPR data-processing agreement. 法兰克福（欧盟）和俄勒冈（美国）。注册时选择区域，之后可以在设置里迁移——迁移只是重新上传密文，谁能读你的笔记这件事不会因此改变。Team 套餐包含 GDPR 数据处理协议。

How do I get all my data out?怎么把我的全部数据导出来？

Your vault already is the export — a folder of .md files and attachments on your disk. For a portable copy, File → Export Vault produces one zip. Individual notes export to PDF and Epub. There is no "request your archive and wait 48 hours" step, because there's nothing to request. 你的笔记库本身就是导出结果——磁盘上一个装着 .md 文件和附件的文件夹。要一份便携副本，「文件 → 导出笔记库」会生成一个 zip。单条笔记可导出 PDF 和 Epub。没有「申请归档并等待 48 小时」这一步，因为没什么可申请的。

Has the encryption been audited by anyone outside Skypad?加密方案有 Skypad 之外的人审计过吗？

Yes. An independent security firm audits the sync protocol and clients every six months; the full reports — findings, severities, and our fixes — are published unedited at skypad.app/audit. The editor core is open source, so you can also read the encryption code yourself. 有。一家独立安全公司每六个月审计一次同步协议和客户端；完整报告——问题、严重级别和我们的修复——原样发布在 skypad.app/audit。编辑器内核开源，你也可以自己读加密相关的代码。

Editor & platforms.编辑器与平台。

One editor, five platforms, no feature held hostage on any of them. 一个编辑器，五个平台，任何平台都不扣留功能。

Is there an AI assistant in the editor?编辑器里有 AI 助手吗？

No, and there won't be. No autocomplete, no rewriting, no summaries — that's a product decision, not a missing feature. Your words form in your head, in your voice. If you want AI on your notes, the files are plain Markdown; point any tool at them, deliberately, outside the editor. 没有，以后也不会有。没有自动补全、没有改写、没有摘要——这是产品决定，不是功能缺失。你的文字在你脑子里、用你的语气形成。想对笔记用 AI？文件就是纯 Markdown，在编辑器之外、有意识地把任何工具指向它们就行。

Can I import from Notion, Obsidian, Evernote, or Apple Notes?能从 Notion、Obsidian、Evernote 或 Apple 备忘录导入吗？

Yes — File → Import handles all four, plus Bear and Day One. Obsidian vaults need no conversion at all (they're already Markdown folders). The Notion and Evernote importers keep attachments, internal links, and creation dates; a dry-run preview shows what will land where before anything is written. 能——「文件 → 导入」支持这四个，外加 Bear 和 Day One。Obsidian 笔记库完全不需要转换（它本来就是 Markdown 文件夹）。Notion 和 Evernote 导入器保留附件、内部链接和创建日期；写入任何文件之前，预览模式会先展示什么内容会落到哪里。

Does it work fully offline?完全离线能用吗？

Completely — offline is the default state, not a degraded mode. Notes live on disk, search runs locally, and sync simply resumes when you're back online. If two devices edited the same note while offline, you get both versions side by side; we never auto-discard either one. 完全可以——离线是默认状态，不是降级模式。笔记存在磁盘上，搜索在本地运行，恢复联网后同步自动继续。如果两台设备离线时改了同一条笔记，两个版本会并排呈现；我们绝不自动丢弃任何一个。

Are there plugins or themes?有插件或主题吗？

Themes yes — light, dark, and four typography presets. Plugins no: a plugin API would turn the quiet editor into a platform, and every platform eventually grows a notification system. What we offer instead is the open format — your .md files work with the whole Markdown tool world outside the app. 主题有——浅色、深色，外加四套排版预设。插件没有：插件 API 会把安静的编辑器变成一个平台，而每个平台最终都会长出通知系统。我们给的替代方案是开放格式——你的 .md 文件可以和应用之外整个 Markdown 工具世界协作。

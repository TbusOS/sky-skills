Local-first · Skypad本地优先 · Skypad

Your notes live on your disk. 笔记在你的磁盘上。

Writing is offline by default. Cloud sync is an option you turn on — encrypted so Skypad itself can't read your notes. Format is plain Markdown, openable 20 years from now by any text editor. 默认离线写作。云同步是可选项 —— 开启后端到端加密，Skypad 自己看不到内容。格式是普通 Markdown，20 年后任何文本编辑器都能打开。

  • Offline is the default, not a fallback.离线是默认状态，不是降级。
  • Vendor going under → notes still on your disk.厂商倒闭 → 笔记还在你的磁盘。
  • Plain Markdown: grep, git, any editor.普通 Markdown：grep、git、任何编辑器。
  • No vendor reads your content.厂商读不到你的内容。

Cloud-native · Notion / Evernote云端原生 · Notion / 印象笔记

Your notes live on a server. 笔记在服务器上。

Source of truth is the server. Every device renders the same live state. Collaboration is native — two people on the same page see each other typing. The trade-off: you trust the vendor and the vendor's uptime. 服务器是真实源。每台设备渲染同一份实时状态。多人协作是原生能力 —— 两人同改一页时互相看到光标。代价：你信任厂商、信任它的在线时间。

  • Real-time collaboration with zero plumbing.多人实时协作，零配置。
  • Rich blocks beyond text (tables, databases, embeds).远超纯文本的富块（表格、数据库、嵌入）。
  • Same state across devices, always.多设备状态始终一致。
  • Vendor-backed search, permissions, admin.厂商提供搜索、权限、管理。

Three clear scenarios. 三个清晰的场景。

Most decisions collapse to one of these. 大多数选择落在这三种里的一种。

Pick Skypad选 Skypad

Solo thinker, long time horizon. 独立思考者，长时间跨度。

A journal, reading log, research vault. You want those notes readable in 20 years, searchable without waiting on someone's servers, not tied to a subscription you might stop paying. Local-first is the only pattern that passes all three tests. 日记、读书笔记、研究素材库。你希望 20 年后还能读到、搜索不用等服务器、不被订阅费绑架。本地优先是唯一三项全过的模式。

Pick Notion / Evernote选 Notion / 印象

Team editing the same page daily. 团队每天共编同一页。

Five teammates editing the roadmap at the same time. Comment threads, permissions, admin history. If real-time collaboration is the core workflow, the cloud's native advantage wins — local-first can do it with CRDTs, but cloud-native gives it to you with zero setup. 五位同事同时改同一张 roadmap。评论线、权限、管理后台。实时协作就是核心工作流时，云端原生的零配置优势压倒 —— 本地优先能用 CRDT 做到，但不是零成本。

Either works两者皆可

Short-term project, low stakes. 短期项目，低风险。

A trip itinerary, a week-long shopping list, a recipe collection you'll clone once and never touch again. Either side serves this fine — pick whichever you already have open. Long-term trade-offs don't compound on a two-week workload. 一次出行行程、一周购物清单、从博客克隆来再也不改的菜谱。两边都够用 —— 哪个已经开着就用哪个。两周的工作量撑不出长期权衡。

The best tool is the one that matches your actual work — not the one with the better marketing. 最好的工具，是和你真正工作匹配的那个 —— 不是营销做得更好的那个。

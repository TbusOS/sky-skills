iPhone 17 Pro: the ultimate iPhone

September 9, 2025

Today Apple introduced iPhone 17 Pro and iPhone 17 Pro Max — the most advanced iPhone models ever — featuring a stunning new titanium design, the A19 Pro chip, and a reimagined Pro Fusion camera system.

A new camera system

The Pro Fusion camera system pairs a 48MP Main camera with an upgraded 5x Telephoto and a new 14mm Ultra Wide. A redesigned image pipeline, powered by the A19 Pro Neural Engine, produces more natural skin tones and a deeper sense of depth, particularly in low light.

For the first time, iPhone captures ProRes RAW directly to external storage over USB 3. Creators can pair iPhone 17 Pro with a supported SSD and record in 4K at 120 fps, edit on the go in Final Cut Camera, and keep working when they get back to a Mac.

“The most advanced iPhone we’ve ever designed.” Greg Joswiak, Apple’s senior vice president of Worldwide Marketing

Built for Apple Intelligence

The A19 Pro chip brings a 50% faster Neural Engine and up to 12GB of unified memory, so iPhone 17 Pro can run larger on-device models locally. Developers can tap new Foundation Model APIs to build private, responsive, and deeply integrated intelligence into their apps.

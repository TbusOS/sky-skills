Why we removed the AI assistant we shipped six months ago. 为什么我们撤掉了半年前刚上线的 AI 助手。

It worked. Customers asked for it. We shipped it well. Then we read three months of usage logs side by side with the writing the assistant produced, and decided we'd been wrong about what the product was for. This is what we found and how we walked it back. 它能用。客户在要。我们做得也不差。然后我们把三个月的使用数据和它产出的文字摆在一起读了一遍，发现我们对这个产品的方向判断错了。这是我们看到了什么，以及我们怎么把它撤回来。

A short defense of three-key chords. 三键连按：一份简短的辩护。

Two-key shortcuts run out fast. Single-key launchers steal whole letters. Three-key chords feel slow on day one and disappear on day three. The middle path holds. 双键组合很快就用光了。单键 launcher 又会抢走字母。三键连按第一天觉得慢，第三天就忘了它的存在。中间这条路最稳。

Why we kept the rendered view, but not the WYSIWYG. 为什么我们保留了"渲染视图"，但没做 WYSIWYG。

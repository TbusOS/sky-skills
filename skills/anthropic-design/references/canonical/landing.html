Why Skypad

Three decisions we're not compromising on.

The features most note apps advertise are exactly the ones we declined to build. Here's what we chose instead.

Local-first

Your notes are plain Markdown files.

Every note you write is a .md file on your own disk. No proprietary format, no database, no lock-in. Open one in VS Code, Obsidian, or cat — they're just text.

How local-first works
No AI

An editor that stops trying to finish your sentence.

We don't ship AI autocomplete, AI rewriting, or AI summaries. Your ideas are yours, formed in your own head, in your own words. If you want AI, use it on purpose — don't let it colonize your thinking.

Why we said no
E2E encrypted

We can't read your notes. By design.

Your notes are encrypted on your device before sync. Skypad servers only ever see ciphertext. We publish an open-source security audit every six months; the latest one is at /security.

Read the latest audit
How it feels

Open it. Think. Close it.

No onboarding flow, no template gallery, no "welcome, tell us about yourself." The app opens to a blank page and a cursor. That's the product.

Three keyboard shortcuts. Nothing else to learn.

  • ⌘ N — new note (no form)
  • ⌘ K — quick switcher (any note, any backlink)
  • ⌘ E — toggle writing mode (hides everything else)

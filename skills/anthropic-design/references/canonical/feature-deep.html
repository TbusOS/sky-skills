Skypad is designed like a terminal: six chord-shortcuts cover 90% of what you do, a command palette indexes everything else, and every motion is one muscle memory away. No menu bar to reach for. No right-click to learn. Skypad 设计得像终端:六个快捷键覆盖 90% 的操作,一个命令面板索引剩下的一切。每一步都近在一个肌肉记忆。不用去点菜单栏,也不用学右键菜单。

The premise前提

Mice are for pointing. Ideas are for typing. 鼠标是用来指的。想法是用来敲的。

A mouse moves your hand off the home row, breaks the thought, forces a micro-decision (where did I put that panel?), and costs a half-second of recovery every time. For a tool whose only job is to stay out of your way, that adds up fast.

鼠标让手离开 home row,打断思路,每次都要做一次微决策("那个面板在哪来着?"),之后还要半秒钟回神。对一个只需要"不挡路"的工具来说,这些成本很快会累积。

Skypad's shortcuts aren't mnemonic tricks. They were chosen by counting, across a year of writing logs, which actions we reached for most often and then giving those actions chords that a touch-typist's fingers already know how to make. Most of them work with one hand.

Skypad 的快捷键不是助记技巧。它们是从一年的写作日志里统计出最常用的动作,然后把这些动作分配给触键者的手指本来就熟悉的组合。大多数可以一只手按。

The best interface is the one you forget is there. A keyboard that already fits your fingers is closer to invisible than any menu bar. 最好的界面,是你忘记它存在的那一种。一套已经贴合你手指的键盘,比任何菜单栏都更接近"看不见"。

The six chords这六个快捷键

What you will use every day. 你每天都会用的那几个。

Find · cmd-K查找 · cmd-K
⌘+K

Open command palette打开命令面板

Your index into every note, every command, every setting. Fuzzy-matches filenames, headings, and recent edits. The one chord you can't do without. 所有笔记、命令、设置的总索引。模糊匹配文件名、标题、最近编辑。这一个少不了。

Jump · cmd-O跳转 · cmd-O
⌘+O

Jump to any note跳到任意笔记

File-only variant of the palette. Same fuzzy match, no commands — ideal when you already know the note and want the fewest keystrokes to get there. 只搜文件的简化版。一样的模糊匹配,不含命令 —— 适合你已经知道要哪篇笔记、想用最少键击到达的时候。

New · cmd-N新建 · cmd-N
⌘+N

New note, current folder在当前目录新建笔记

Creates a note in the folder you're looking at, named by timestamp. Type over the name if you want. The cursor lands in the body, not the filename. 在当前文件夹下按时间戳新建。想改名直接敲。光标落在正文里,不在文件名上。

Focus · cmd-⇧-F专注 · cmd-⇧-F
⌘+⇧+F

Focus mode专注模式

Hides the file tree, the sidebar, the status bar. Just the note, centred at 680px. Press again to bring the scaffolding back. 隐藏文件树、侧栏、状态栏。只剩那篇笔记,居中 680px 宽。再按一次把脚手架找回来。

Link · double-[链接 · 双 [
[+[

Link to another note链到另一篇笔记

Opens an inline picker for the note you want to link to. Tab to accept, Esc to cancel. The link becomes bidirectional — the target gets a back-reference automatically. 打开一个行内的选择器,挑你想链过去的笔记。Tab 确认,Esc 取消。链接是双向的 —— 目标笔记会自动得到一个反向引用。

Toggle · cmd-E切换 · cmd-E
⌘+E

Preview ↔ source预览 ↔ 源码

Switch between rendered Markdown and raw source. Cursor position is preserved across the toggle, so you can flip back and edit exactly where you were reading. 在渲染后的 Markdown 和源码之间切换。光标位置跨切换保留,读到哪里就能在哪里继续改。

The palette命令面板

Everything else lives in one box. 其他一切,都住在一个框里。

Press ⌘K and a single input takes over the screen. What you type fuzzy-matches against four namespaces at once: 按 ⌘K,一个输入框占据整个屏幕。你敲的字符同时对四个命名空间做模糊匹配:

  • Notes. Every filename and H1 across your vault. 笔记。 仓库里所有文件名和 H1。
  • Recent. The 20 files you touched in the last hour, boosted in the ranking. 最近。 最近一小时碰过的 20 个文件,排序加权。
  • Commands. Every menu item, every setting, every action that could be bound to a key. 命令。 每一个菜单项、每一个设置、每一个可以绑定到按键的动作。
  • Headings. Any ## or deeper inside the file you're currently reading. 标题。 你当前文件里的任何 ## 或更深层级标题。

Enter to open, ⇧-Enter to open in a split pane, ⌥-Enter to copy its Markdown link to the clipboard. Enter 打开,⇧-Enter 在分栏里打开,⌥-Enter 把它的 Markdown 链接复制到剪贴板。

Honest caveat坦诚的边界

Three things we still reach for a mouse to do. 还有三件事,我们也还要伸手去摸鼠标。

Dragging a file tree reorder, scrubbing through a long note, and resizing a split pane are all faster with a pointer than with any keybinding we have come up with. We have not tried to make these keyboard-only, because forcing them to be would break the rule that the fastest hand wins. 拖拽文件树、在长笔记里滑动、拖改分栏宽度 —— 这三件用鼠标都比任何我们能想出来的键位快。我们没有硬塞键盘快捷键,因为那会违反"哪只手更快就用哪只"这条规则。

Technical details技术细节

If you care about the how. 如果你在意怎么做到的。

Palette latency面板响应

Median 3.2ms from keypress to first result on a 20,000-note vault, measured on an M1 MacBook Air with the vault fully cold (no pre-warmed cache). The fuzzy matcher is incremental — we reuse last frame's score table when the query extends by one character. 在一个 20,000 篇笔记的仓库上,从按键到第一个结果中位 3.2ms(M1 MacBook Air,仓库完全冷启动,无预热缓存)。模糊匹配是增量的 —— 当 query 多一个字符时,我们重用上一帧的打分表。

Keyboard layouts键盘布局

QWERTY, Dvorak, Colemak, and AZERTY. Chords are bound to physical keys, not logical letters — ⌘K on a Dvorak keyboard is the physical key where QWERTY's K would be, not Dvorak's mapped letter. This keeps muscle memory portable when you switch layouts. 支持 QWERTY、Dvorak、Colemak、AZERTY。快捷键绑的是物理键位,不是逻辑字母 —— Dvorak 上的 ⌘K 就是 QWERTY K 的物理位置,不是 Dvorak 映射出的字母。切换布局时肌肉记忆不需要重学。

Platform coverage平台覆盖

macOS, Windows, Linux (deb + rpm + flatpak + AppImage). The chord table is identical across platforms — ⌘ becomes Ctrl, ⌥ becomes Alt. No platform has a special shortcut you have to remember differently. macOS、Windows、Linux(deb + rpm + flatpak + AppImage)。快捷键表各平台一致 —— ⌘ 换成 Ctrl,⌥ 换成 Alt。没有哪个平台有"特殊"快捷键需要你单独记。

Rebindable?可不可以改?

Yes. Every chord is listed in ~/.skypad/keys.toml and can be rebound to any free combination. The one we suggest you leave alone is ⌘K — the palette is the escape hatch for every other action, so losing it costs more than any custom chord gains. 可以。所有快捷键列在 ~/.skypad/keys.toml 里,可以改绑到任何空闲组合。建议你不要动的只有一个:⌘K —— 因为命令面板是所有其他动作的退出通道,丢了它的损失大于任何自定义快捷键能带来的增益。

The team团队

Nine people.
Three cities.
One quiet product. 九个人。
三座城市。
一件安静的产品。

Skypad is made by nine people in Berlin, Toronto and Kyoto. No managers, no growth targets, almost no meetings. We keep the team small for the same reason we keep the product quiet: fewer moving parts, more attention per decision. Skypad 由柏林、多伦多、京都的九个人做出来。没有经理，没有增长指标，几乎没有会议。我们把团队保持得很小，理由和把产品做得安静是同一个：更少的活动部件，每个决定分到更多注意力。

How we work工作方式

Three habits that keep it calm.让一切保持安静的三个习惯。

A quiet product doesn't come out of a loud company. These three habits are how the team stays as calm as the editor. 安静的产品不会出自一家吵闹的公司。靠这三个习惯，团队和编辑器一样安静。

Async first异步优先

Write it down.写下来。

Decisions live in RFC notes, not in meetings. Anyone can read why we chose something three years later — because it's a Markdown file, not a memory. 决定写在 RFC 笔记里，不在会议里。三年后任何人都能查到我们当初为什么这么选——因为那是一个 Markdown 文件，不是某个人的记忆。

How an RFC shipsRFC 是怎么走到上线的
Small on purpose故意保持小

Nine people. No managers.九个人。没有经理。

We hire when a job starts to hurt, not when a spreadsheet says we should. Small keeps every person close to the product and every decision close to a user. 我们在某项工作开始吃紧时才招人，而不是在表格说该招时招人。小，让每个人都离产品很近，每个决定都离用户很近。

When we hire我们什么时候招人
Our own vault自己的仓库

The handbook is a Skypad vault.手册就是一个 Skypad 仓库。

Every RFC, every weekly note, every page of the public handbook is a plain Markdown file in a shared vault. If Skypad annoys us, we feel it before you do. 每一篇 RFC、每一条周记、公开手册的每一页，都是共享仓库里的普通 Markdown 文件。如果 Skypad 让人烦，我们会比你先受不了。

Read the handbook阅读员工手册

There is no hour when all nine of us are at work. 没有哪个小时，九个人同时在工作。

Berlin, Toronto and Kyoto share at most two working hours a day — and between Kyoto and Toronto the overlap is exactly zero. 柏林、多伦多、京都每天最多共享两个工作小时——而京都和多伦多之间的重叠恰好是零。

That constraint is where the RFC habit comes from. Anything that matters gets written down, because the person who needs it next is usually asleep right now. RFC 这个习惯正是被这个约束逼出来的。重要的事都写下来，因为下一个需要它的人，此刻多半在睡觉。

Shipping交付

From idea to changelog.从想法到更新日志。

Every change follows the same written path. The only branch is for code that touches sync or encryption — that goes through an external review before it ships. 每个改动都走同一条书面路径。唯一的分支留给涉及同步或加密的代码——它们要先通过外部评审才能上线。

We didn't set out to stay small. We set out to stay calm — small is just what calm looks like in practice. 我们没有立志保持小。我们立志保持安静——小，只是安静落到实处的样子。

Hiring招聘

Work with us.和我们一起工作。

We hire about twice a year, when a job starts to hurt. New roles appear in the changelog before anywhere else. If nothing is open, write to us anyway — we read every letter, and we answer. 我们大约一年招两次人，在某项工作开始吃紧的时候。新职位会先出现在更新日志里。就算现在没有空缺，也欢迎来信——每一封我们都读，而且都会回。

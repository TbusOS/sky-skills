The two truths两种事实

Neither side is a compromise version of the other. 两边都不是对方的打折版。

Each was designed fully from its first principles. Read both claims honestly. 各自都是从第一性原理完整设计的产物。两边的主张都诚实地读一遍。

Local-first · Skypad本地优先 · Skypad

Your notes live on your disk. Sync is optional.笔记在你的磁盘上。同步是可选项。

Writing is offline by default. Cloud sync is an optional feature you turn on — and when you do, it's end-to-end encrypted so Skypad itself can't read your notes. The data format is plain Markdown files, openable by any text editor, 20 years from now. 默认离线写作。云同步是你主动打开的可选项 —— 打开后也是端到端加密,Skypad 自己看不到你的内容。数据就是普通 Markdown 文件,任何文本编辑器都能打开,20 年后也能。

  • Offline is the default, not a degraded mode.离线是默认状态,不是降级模式。
  • Vendor going under = your notes are still on your disk.厂商倒闭 = 你的笔记还在你的磁盘上。
  • Plain Markdown = grep, git, any editor works.普通 Markdown = grep、git、任何编辑器都能用。
  • No vendor can read your content.厂商读不到你的内容。
Cloud-native · Notion / Evernote云端原生 · Notion / 印象笔记

Your notes live on a server. Clients are windows into it.笔记在服务器上。客户端是通向它的窗口。

The source of truth is the server. Every device renders the same live state. Collaboration is native — two people editing the same page see each other typing. The trade-off: you trust the vendor, you need an account, and when the vendor is down, so is your notebook. 服务器是唯一真实源。每个设备渲染同一份实时状态。多人协作是原生能力 —— 两人同改一页时互相看到光标。代价:你信任厂商、需要账号,厂商宕机时你的笔记本也宕机。

  • Real-time collaboration with zero plumbing.多人实时协作,零配置。
  • Rich block types beyond plain text (tables, databases, embeds).远超纯文本的富块类型(表格、数据库、嵌入)。
  • Same state across devices, always.多设备状态始终一致。
  • Vendor-backed search, permissions, admin tools.厂商提供搜索、权限、管理工具。

Pick the right one选对那一个

Three clear scenarios. 三个清晰的场景。

Skip the pro/con matrix. The decision usually collapses to one of these. 跳过长长的优劣表格。选择通常就落在这三种里的一种。

Pick Skypad选 Skypad

Solo thinker, long time horizon 独立思考者 · 长时间跨度

You keep a journal, a reading log, or a research vault. You want those notes readable in 20 years, searchable without waiting on anyone's servers, and not tied to a subscription you might stop paying. Local-first is the only pattern that survives all three tests. 你在记日记、读书笔记或研究素材库。你希望 20 年后还能读到它们、搜索时不用等服务器、也不要被订阅费绑架。本地优先是唯一三项都过关的模式。

Pick Notion / Evernote选 Notion / 印象

Team editing the same page daily 团队每天共编同一页

Five teammates editing the roadmap at the same time. Comments thread, permissions, admin history. Local-first can do this with CRDTs and some plumbing, but cloud-native gives it to you with zero setup. If real-time collab is the core workflow, the cloud's native advantage wins. 五位同事同时改同一张 roadmap。评论线、权限、管理后台。本地优先可以用 CRDT 加配置做到,但云端原生零配置就给你了。如果实时协作就是核心工作流,云端原生的优势压倒。

Either works两者皆可

Short-term project, low stakes 短期项目 · 低风险

A trip itinerary, a week-long shopping list, a recipe collection you'll clone from a blog and never edit again. Either side serves this fine — pick whichever you already have open. Long-term trade-offs don't compound on a two-week workload. 一次出行行程、一周购物清单、从博客克隆来再也不改的菜谱。两边都够用 —— 哪个已经开着就用哪个。两周的工作量撑不出长期权衡。

Honest closing诚实的结尾
Skypad is local-first. That's a trade-off, not a universal winner. If collaboration is your main workflow, use Notion. If you want notes you'll still be reading in 20 years, use us. The worst tool is the one you chose by following a trend — match the tool to the work, not to the marketing.

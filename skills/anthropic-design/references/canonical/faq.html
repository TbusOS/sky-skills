Pricing & billing定价与账单

What it costs, and what it doesn't.要花什么钱，不要花什么钱。

The full price list is three lines: Free for solo, Pro $9/month, Team $29/seat/month. These five answers cover everything around those three lines. 完整价目表只有三行：单人免费、Pro $9/月、Team $29/席位/月。下面五个回答覆盖这三行周围的一切。

Is Free really free forever, or a trial in disguise?免费版真的永久免费，还是变相试用？▸

Free forever, and not a trial. The Free plan is the full editor — unlimited notes, Markdown + MDX, export to PDF, Epub and Markdown — running entirely on your machine, no account required. What you pay for on Pro is encrypted sync between devices. If you write on one machine, you may never need to pay us anything. 永久免费，不是试用。免费版就是完整的编辑器 —— 无限笔记、Markdown + MDX、导出 PDF/Epub/Markdown —— 全部在你本机运行，无需注册账号。Pro 收费的部分是跨设备加密同步。如果你只在一台机器上写作，可能永远不需要付我们一分钱。

What exactly do I get for $9 on Pro?Pro 的 $9 到底买到什么？▸

Encrypted cloud sync across 5 devices, unlimited backlinks and the graph view, 90 days of version history, the iOS and Android apps, and publishing any note to name.skypad.page. Pro is $9/month billed yearly, with a 14-day trial — no card up front. 跨 5 台设备的加密云同步、无限反向链接和关系图、90 天版本历史、iOS 和 Android app，以及把任意笔记发布到 name.skypad.page。Pro $9/月、按年付费，有 14 天试用 —— 试用不需要信用卡。

Do I lose my notes if I cancel Pro?取消 Pro 后会丢笔记吗？▸

No. Every note is a plain Markdown file on your own disk — cancelling changes nothing about the files. Sync stops, version history trims to 7 days, and the mobile apps go read-only. Resubscribe later and sync picks up where it left off. 不会。每条笔记都是你磁盘上的纯 Markdown 文件 —— 取消订阅不改变文件本身的任何东西。同步停止，版本历史缩到 7 天，移动端变为只读。以后重新订阅，同步会从停下的地方继续。

Is there a student or low-income discount?有学生或低收入折扣吗？▸

Pro is $3/month if you send a photo of your student ID, or any evidence you're on a public-benefit program. No application form, no review committee — people who need the discount shouldn't have to prove it twice. Pro 是 $3/月 —— 发一张学生证照片，或任何能证明你在公共福利项目里的材料即可。没有申请表，没有评审委员会：需要折扣的人不该被要求证明两次。

How does Team billing handle people joining mid-month?Team 账单怎么处理月中加入的成员？▸

Seats are prorated by the day: someone joining on the 20th costs a third of that month. Removed seats stay active until the end of the billing cycle, then drop off the invoice. And before you commit at all, the Team plan has a 30-day pilot for up to 5 seats, no credit card — email team@skypad.page. 席位按天折算：20 号加入的人，当月只收三分之一。移除的席位用到当个账单周期结束，下期发票自动减去。在正式购买之前，Team 还有 30 天试点，最多 5 个席位，不需要信用卡 —— 发邮件到 team@skypad.page。

Notes, sync & devices笔记、同步与设备

Where your words live, and how they travel.你的文字存在哪里，怎么移动。

Local-first means the boring failure modes — lost connection, dead startup, conflicting edits — have boring, predictable answers. 本地优先意味着那些无聊的故障场景 —— 断网、公司倒闭、编辑冲突 —— 都有无聊而可预期的答案。

Where do my notes actually live?我的笔记到底存在哪里？▸

In a folder of .md files on your disk — ~/Skypad by default, or any folder you point it at, including one inside Dropbox or iCloud Drive if that's your thing. There is no proprietary database. Open the folder in VS Code or Obsidian and everything is just there. 在你磁盘上的一个 .md 文件夹里 —— 默认是 ~/Skypad，也可以指到任何文件夹，包括 Dropbox 或 iCloud Drive 里的。没有私有数据库。用 VS Code 或 Obsidian 打开那个文件夹，所有东西就在那里。

What happens if two devices edit the same note offline?两台设备离线时改了同一条笔记，会怎样？▸

We keep both versions. The later sync creates a sibling file — meditations (conflict 2026-06-08).md — next to the original, and the app shows the two side by side, so you merge by reading, not by trusting an algorithm. Skypad never silently merges or discards an edit. 两个版本都保留。后同步的那台会在原文件旁边生成 meditations (conflict 2026-06-08).md，应用把两版并排展示，由你读着合并，而不是信任某个算法。Skypad 从不悄悄合并或丢弃任何修改。

Does Skypad work fully offline?Skypad 能完全离线用吗？▸

Completely. Free never opens a network connection at all. On Pro, edits queue locally and sync when you're back online — a flight or a week off-grid changes nothing about how the editor behaves. 完全可以。免费版根本不会发起网络连接。Pro 上，修改先在本地排队，联网后再同步 —— 一趟航班或一周没有网络，编辑器的行为不会有任何变化。

What counts as one of my 5 Pro devices?Pro 的 5 台设备是怎么算的？▸

Any install signed into your vault: Mac, Windows, Linux, iPhone, Android — each counts as one. You can deauthorize an old device from Settings → Devices at any time, which frees the slot immediately. Published name.skypad.page pages don't count; readers aren't devices. 登录了你保险库的任何安装实例都算一台：Mac、Windows、Linux、iPhone、Android。随时可以在「设置 → 设备」里注销旧设备，名额立即释放。发布出去的 name.skypad.page 页面不占名额 —— 读者不是设备。

Security & privacy安全与隐私

What we can't see, and why we can't.我们看不到什么，为什么看不到。

"End-to-end encrypted" is the claim people rightly trust least, so here it is as a picture first, then as five answers. 「端到端加密」是大家最有理由不轻信的说法，所以先画给你看，再用五个回答讲清楚。

What does "end-to-end encrypted" actually mean here?这里说的「端到端加密」具体指什么？▸

Exactly what the diagram above shows. Your note is encrypted on your device with XChaCha20-Poly1305, using a key derived from your passphrase — before a single byte leaves the machine. Skypad's servers store and forward ciphertext; the key never travels. Your other device derives the same key from the same passphrase and decrypts locally. 就是上图画的那样。笔记在你的设备上用 XChaCha20-Poly1305 加密，密钥从你的口令派生 —— 在任何一个字节离开本机之前。Skypad 服务器只存储和转发密文，密钥从不上路。你的另一台设备用同一口令派生出同一把密钥，在本地解密。

Can Skypad employees read my notes?Skypad 的员工能读我的笔记吗？▸

We can't — and "can't" is doing real work in that sentence. The servers hold ciphertext, encrypted filenames, sizes and timestamps; no keys, no plaintext, no titles. A subpoena gets the same ciphertext the diagram shows. This is also why we can't "just restore" your notes if you lose your passphrase. 读不到 —— 这里的「读不到」是技术意义上的，不是公司承诺。服务器上只有密文、加密后的文件名、大小和时间戳；没有密钥，没有明文，没有标题。法院传票拿到的也是上图里那份密文。这也是为什么口令丢了我们没办法「帮你恢复」。

What if I forget my passphrase?忘记口令了怎么办？▸

During setup, Skypad generates a one-time recovery kit — a printable page with a 24-word code. That kit is the only way back in. We can't reset the passphrase for you, because resetting would require us to hold a key, and the entire design is that we don't. Print the kit. Keep it wherever your passport lives. 初次设置时，Skypad 会生成一次性的恢复套件 —— 一页可打印的 24 词代码。忘记口令时，这份套件是唯一的回路。我们没法替你重置口令：重置意味着我们得持有密钥，而整个设计就是我们不持有。把它打印出来，放在你放护照的地方。

Who audits Skypad, and how often?谁来审计 Skypad，多久一次？▸

An independent security firm audits the sync protocol and the desktop and mobile clients every 6 months, and the full report — findings, severity, fixes — is published at skypad.page/security. The latest audit closed in March 2026 with zero critical findings. We have published every report since the first, including the awkward ones. 一家独立安全公司每 6 个月审计一次同步协议和桌面、移动客户端，完整报告 —— 发现、级别、修复 —— 发布在 skypad.page/security。最近一次审计于 2026 年 3 月结束，0 个严重项。从第一份起，每份报告都公开，包括不体面的那几份。

Is Skypad GDPR-compliant? Where are the servers?Skypad 符合 GDPR 吗？服务器在哪里？▸

Sync servers run in Frankfurt, Germany, under EU jurisdiction. We're GDPR-compliant the boring way: minimal metadata, a signed DPA for Team customers, and deletion that actually deletes — close your account and the ciphertext is purged within 30 days. Team plans can additionally pin data residency to the EU or US region. 同步服务器在德国法兰克福，受欧盟司法管辖。我们用最无聊的方式符合 GDPR：最少化的元数据、给 Team 客户的签字 DPA，以及真正删除的删除 —— 注销账号后，密文 30 天内清除。Team 套餐还可以把数据驻留固定在欧盟或美国区。

June 20262026 年 6 月

v2.4.0 Jun 4, 20266 月 4 日

Skypad goes mobile.Skypad 移动端来了。

The same vault, on your phone. Notes open offline, stay encrypted, and sync only when you ask them to — exactly like the desktop app. 同一个 vault，装进你的手机。笔记离线打开、保持加密、只在你要求时才同步——和桌面版完全一样。

  • Added新增Skypad for iOS and Android. Full vault, offline-first; a 4,000-note vault opens in under a second on a five-year-old phone.Skypad iOS 与 Android 版。完整 vault，离线优先；4,000 条笔记的 vault 在五年前的手机上一秒内打开。
  • Added新增Per-device keys. Each phone holds its own keypair — revoke a lost device from any other device, without re-encrypting the vault.设备独立密钥。每台手机持有自己的密钥对——丢了设备，从任何其他设备上吊销即可，无需重新加密整个 vault。
  • Changed调整Sync protocol v3 batches small edits. A typical save now sends 3 KB instead of 18 KB.同步协议 v3 会合并小编辑。一次普通保存现在只发 3 KB，原来是 18 KB。
  • Fixed修复Writing-mode cursor no longer jumps when an external keyboard is attached on iPad.iPad 外接键盘时，写作模式光标不再乱跳。

May 20262026 年 5 月

v2.3.2 May 27, 20265 月 27 日

Three fixes, no surprises.三个修复，没有意外。

  • Fixed修复CJK input — composing pinyin in writing mode no longer drops the first candidate when an autosave fires mid-composition.中文输入——写作模式下拼音组词时若恰逢自动保存，首个候选词不再丢失。
  • Fixed修复The Linux file watcher now follows symlinked vault folders; external edits show up without a restart.Linux 文件监听现在能跟踪软链接的 vault 目录；外部编辑无需重启即可出现。

v2.3.1 May 13, 20265 月 13 日

Quick-switcher tune-up.快速跳转的微调。

  • Fixed修复⌘K fuzzy match treats hyphens and spaces the same, so "on-solitude" matches "on solitude".⌘K 模糊匹配现在把连字符和空格视为等同，「on-solitude」可以匹配「on solitude」。
  • Changed调整The switcher ranks recently edited notes above exact-prefix matches. Your last three notes are almost always what you want.跳转列表把最近编辑的笔记排在前缀精确匹配之前。你要找的，几乎总是最近写的那三条。
v2.3.0 May 6, 20265 月 6 日

Backlinks, and search operators.反向链接，和搜索操作符。

Two features writers kept asking for — built so they stay out of the way until you reach for them. 两个作者们一直在要的功能——做成不去找它们就不会出现的样子。

  • Added新增Backlinks panel. Every note lists the notes that reference it; ⌘E hides it along with everything else.反向链接面板。每条笔记列出引用它的笔记；⌘E 会连同其他一切一起把它藏起来。
  • Added新增Search operators: path:reading, before:2026-01, has:todo. Plain words still work exactly as before.搜索操作符：path:reading、before:2026-01、has:todo。普通词语照旧可用。
  • Changed调整The search index updates incrementally on save. A 12,000-note vault re-indexes in 0.4 s instead of 40 s.搜索索引随保存增量更新。12,000 条笔记的 vault 重建索引从 40 秒降到 0.4 秒。

How we ship我们怎么发布

Three rules this page lives by.这页遵守的三条规则。

1

No breaking changes outside a major version.大版本之外，不做破坏性变更。

Your vault is plain Markdown on your disk. Nothing we ship in a 2.x release will ever change how those files are read — that promise only moves at v3.0, with a migration note published first.你的 vault 是磁盘上的普通 Markdown。任何 2.x 发布都不会改变这些文件的读取方式——这条承诺只在 v3.0 才会动，而且会先发迁移说明。

2

Confirmed data bugs get a patch within 48 hours.确认的数据 bug，48 小时内出补丁。

Anything that can lose or corrupt a note skips the release train. v2.3.2's CJK composition fix went from report to release in 41 hours.任何可能丢失或损坏笔记的问题都不等发布班车。v2.3.2 的中文组词修复，从收到报告到发布用了 41 小时。

3

Every note here is written by the engineer who shipped it.每条说明都由发布它的工程师亲手写。

No marketing pass, no adjectives without numbers. If an entry says “faster”, it says how much, measured on what.没有市场部润色，没有不带数字的形容词。如果某条写了「更快」，就一定写明快了多少、在什么环境下测的。

Never miss a release.不错过任何一次发布。

One email per release, written like the notes above. No product news, no surveys, unsubscribe in one click. 每次发布一封邮件，写法和上面的说明一样。没有产品营销，没有问卷，一键退订。

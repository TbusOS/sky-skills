FAQ

Questions worth asking.值得问的问题。

Do I lose my notes if I cancel Pro?取消 Pro 后会丢笔记吗?

No. Every note you ever wrote is a plain Markdown file on your own machine — that's the local-first in local-first. If you cancel, sync stops and version history trims to 7 days, but your notes stay exactly where they were. Export runs the same on Free. 不会。你写过的每一条笔记都是本机上的纯 Markdown 文件 —— 这就是"本地优先"里的"本地"。取消后同步停止，版本历史缩到 7 天，但笔记还在原地。导出功能在免费版上一样用。

What does "end-to-end encrypted" actually mean?"端到端加密" 到底什么意思?

Your notes are encrypted on your device with a key derived from your passphrase, before they leave your machine. Skypad servers store ciphertext; we can't read your notes, and we can't recover them if you lose your passphrase. We ship an open-source audit report every 6 months — the latest one is linked from /security. 你的笔记在设备上用从你密码派生的密钥加密后才离开本机。Skypad 服务器只存密文；我们读不到你的笔记，密码丢了也恢复不了。每 6 个月发一份开源审计报告，最新那份链接在 /security。

Is there an AI-assist feature?有 AI 辅助写作吗?

No — and that's a product decision, not a roadmap item. The reason people stay on Skypad is that it's the one place that isn't trying to complete their sentences. If you want AI in your editor, Skypad isn't for you, and we're not upset about it. 没有 —— 而且这是产品决定，不是待开发项。人们留在 Skypad 的原因恰恰是：这是一个不试图替你补完句子的地方。如果你想要编辑器里有 AI，Skypad 不是给你的，我们对此没有什么需要道歉。

Can my team try it before committing to 10 seats?我的团队能先试再买 10 个座位吗?

Yes. The Team plan has a 30-day pilot for up to 5 seats, no credit card. Email team@skypad.page with your company and we'll set it up within one business day. 可以。Team 套餐有 30 天试点，最多 5 个座位，不用信用卡。发邮件到 team@skypad.page 写明公司，一个工作日内开通。

I'm a student / on a low income. Discount?我是学生 / 低收入，有折扣吗?

Pro is $3/month if you send us a photo of your student ID, or any evidence you're on a public-benefit program. No application, no review committee. We don't make the form hard — people who need the discount shouldn't also have to prove it twice. Pro 是 $3/月，只要给我们看学生证，或任何能证明你在公共福利项目里的证据。不用填申请，不用审核委员会。我们不把表格做难 —— 需要折扣的人不应该还要多证明一次。

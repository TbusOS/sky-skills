03 · How we decide我们怎么做决定

Every feature starts
as a note. 每个功能，
都从一条笔记开始。

No roadmap meetings. A pitch is written down, sits for a quiet week, gets one call — and most pitches end in the archive. That's the system working. 没有路线图会议。提案先写下来，安静放一周，再开一次电话会——多数提案最后归档。这正说明这套办法在起作用。

04 · What we hold to我们坚持的

Three habits,
seven years old. 三个习惯，
坚持了七年。

Slow is a feature.慢，是功能。

No quarterly roadmap, no growth targets. We ship when a thing is genuinely done, then keep it working for years. Skypad has never had a redesign. 没有季度路线图，没有增长指标。东西真正做完才发布，然后让它好用很多年。Skypad 从没改版过。

Everything is written down.一切都写下来。

We run the company inside Skypad. Decisions, pitches, support patterns — all notes, all linked. With 14 hours between Kyoto and Toronto, writing beats meeting. 公司就运行在 Skypad 里。决策、提案、支持记录——全是笔记，互相链接。京都和多伦多差着 14 个小时，写下来比开会管用。

Everyone answers support.人人都答支持邮件。

Engineers, designers, founders — one shared support rotation, no tiers. The person who built a feature hears, firsthand, exactly where it broke. 工程师、设计师、创始人——同一个支持轮值，不分级别。谁做的功能，谁第一手听到它哪里坏了。

We never wanted Skypad to be a big company. We want it to be a good tool that is still here, unchanged in spirit, in twenty years. 我们从没想把 Skypad 做成一家大公司。我们想让它是一件好工具——二十年后还在，气质未变。
Mara Lindqvist — co-founder · Berlin Mara Lindqvist — 联合创始人 · 柏林

Why we removed the AI assistant we shipped six months ago. 为什么我们撤掉了半年前刚上线的 AI 助手。

It worked. Customers asked for it. We shipped it well. Then we read three months of usage logs side by side with the writing the assistant produced, and decided we'd got the question wrong. This is what we found and how we walked it back. 它能用。客户在要。我们做得也不差。然后我们把三个月的使用数据和它产出的文字摆在一起读了一遍，发现我们一开始问错了问题。这是我们看到了什么，以及我们怎么把它撤回来。

A short defence of three-key chords. 为三键和弦短作一辩。

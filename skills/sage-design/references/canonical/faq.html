02 · Plans & billing套餐与计费

Free, Pro,
and Team. 免费版、Pro
与团队版。

The whole price list is three numbers: free forever, $9 a month for sync, $29 a seat for teams. These six answers cover the edges around them. 整张价目表只有三个数字：永久免费、同步 $9/月、团队 $29/席位。下面六条回答把价目表的边边角角讲清楚。

01

What do I get free, forever?免费版永久包含什么？

The whole editor. Unlimited notes, backlinks, the graph, writing mode, export to PDF and HTML — all of it, on one device, with no account. Free isn't a trial that expires or a quota that runs out; it's the product. Pro exists for exactly one reason: moving your notes between devices, encrypted. 整个编辑器。不限量的笔记、反向链接、关系图、写作模式、导出 PDF 和 HTML —— 全部都有，单台设备，无需账号。免费版不是会过期的试用，也不是会用完的额度，它就是产品本身。Pro 只为一件事存在：把你的笔记加密同步到多台设备。

02

What exactly does Pro add for $9?$9 的 Pro 具体多了什么？

Three things: end-to-end encrypted sync on up to 5 devices, 12 months of version history (Free keeps 7 days), and publishing any note to name.skypad.page. That's the complete list — there are no Pro-only editor features, and there never will be. Yearly is $90, two months free. 三件事：最多 5 台设备的端到端加密同步、12 个月的版本历史(免费版保留 7 天)、把任意笔记发布到 name.skypad.page。清单到此为止 —— 编辑器功能没有任何 Pro 专属，以后也不会有。年付 $90，相当于送两个月。

03

What happens if I cancel?取消订阅会发生什么？

Your notes don't move — every one is a plain .md file already on your disk. Sync stops at the end of the billing period, version history trims back to 7 days, and published pages stay up for 90 days so your readers aren't surprised, then unpublish. Resubscribe any time and everything resumes. 你的笔记原地不动 —— 每一条本来就是你磁盘上的 .md 纯文本文件。同步在计费周期结束时停止，版本历史缩回 7 天，已发布页面会再保留 90 天(免得你的读者措手不及)，然后下线。任何时候重新订阅，一切照旧。

04

Do you offer refunds?可以退款吗？

Yes — 30 days, no questions, no form. Email billing@skypad.page from your account address and a human refunds you within one business day; the money lands back in 3–5 depending on your bank. This applies to yearly plans too, prorated from the day you write. 可以 —— 30 天内，不问原因，没有表单。用你的账号邮箱写信到 billing@skypad.page，一个真人会在一个工作日内办理，钱 3–5 天内回到账上(取决于你的银行)。年付同样适用，从你来信那天起按比例退。

05

I'm a student, or money is tight. Discount?学生或手头紧，有折扣吗？

Pro is $3 a month — send a photo of your student ID, or any evidence you're on a public-benefit program. No application form, no review committee, no annual re-verification. People who need the discount shouldn't have to prove it twice. Pro 降到 $3/月 —— 发一张学生证照片，或任何能说明你在领公共福利的材料即可。没有申请表，没有审核委员会，也不用每年重新验证。需要折扣的人，不该被要求证明两遍。

06

How does Team billing work?团队版怎么计费？

$29 per seat per month; add or remove seats any month and the next invoice adjusts. Before committing, there's a 30-day pilot for up to 5 seats with no credit card — email team@skypad.page and we set it up within one business day. From 10 seats we also do annual invoicing with a PO. $29/席位/月；任何月份都能增减席位，下一张账单自动调整。决定之前可以先试：30 天、最多 5 个席位、无需信用卡 —— 写信到 team@skypad.page，一个工作日内开通。10 个席位起也支持按采购单年付开票。

03 · Sync & devices同步与设备

Local first,
synced second. 本地优先，
同步在后。

Every edit lands on your disk before it goes anywhere. These six answers explain what that means when you're offline, on five devices, or on your way out. 每一次编辑先落到你的磁盘，然后才谈别的。下面六条回答解释：离线时、五台设备时、甚至你想离开时，这意味着什么。

01

Does Skypad work offline?Skypad 能离线用吗？

Always — offline is the default state, not a degraded mode. Every keystroke is saved to the .md file on your disk first; sync is a background process that catches up whenever a connection exists. A week in a cabin costs you nothing but the week. 永远可以 —— 离线是默认状态，不是降级模式。每一次击键都先存进你磁盘上的 .md 文件；同步只是一个后台过程，有网时自己追上。在小木屋里待一周，你失去的只有那一周。

02

How many devices can I sync?最多能同步几台设备？

Five on Pro — say, a laptop, a desktop, a phone, a tablet, and one spare. Each install counts as one device regardless of platform. You can see and deactivate devices under Settings → Devices; deactivating one frees the slot immediately and wipes its sync key. Pro 是五台 —— 比如一台笔记本、一台台式机、一部手机、一台平板，再留一个备用。不分平台，每个安装算一台。在 Settings → Devices 里能查看并停用设备；停用立即释放名额，同时吊销那台设备的同步密钥。

03

What if two devices edit the same note?两台设备同时改同一条笔记怎么办？

We keep both, always. The later edit becomes the main file, and the other version is saved next to it as on-stillness (conflict 2026-06-09).md so you can merge by hand. Skypad never auto-merges prose and never silently overwrites — losing a paragraph you wrote is the one failure we refuse to risk. 两份都留，永远如此。较晚的修改成为主文件，另一份存在旁边，叫 on-stillness (conflict 2026-06-09).md，由你手动合并。Skypad 从不自动合并文章，也从不悄悄覆盖 —— 弄丢你写下的一段话，是我们唯一拒绝冒的险。

04

Can I leave and take everything with me?想走的话，能带走全部笔记吗？

There's nothing to take — it's already yours. A vault is a plain folder of Markdown files; point Obsidian, iA Writer, or grep at it today and they'll read it. No export wizard, no waiting for an archive email, no proprietary format to escape from. The honest answer is that leaving Skypad takes zero steps. 没什么可带走的 —— 它本来就在你手里。Vault 就是一个装满 Markdown 文件的普通文件夹；今天就能用 Obsidian、iA Writer 或者 grep 直接读。没有导出向导，不用等打包邮件，没有要逃离的私有格式。诚实的回答是：离开 Skypad 需要零个步骤。

05

How big can a vault get?Vault 能有多大？

We test against a 40,000-note vault with 2 GB of attachments on every release. A first full sync of that takes about four minutes on an ordinary connection; after that, syncs ship only diffs and finish in seconds. Search stays under 50 ms because the index lives on your machine, not ours. 每个版本发布前，我们都用一个 40,000 条笔记、带 2 GB 附件的 vault 做测试。这样的 vault 首次完整同步在普通网络下约四分钟；之后每次只传增量，几秒内完成。搜索始终在 50 ms 以内，因为索引在你的机器上，不在我们这边。

06

Is there a web version?有网页版吗？

No, and it's deliberate. A web editor can't keep the local-first promise — your notes would have to live in a browser sandbox we control, and "local" would become marketing. The only web part of Skypad is the pages you choose to publish. The apps are native on macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android. 没有，而且是有意的。网页编辑器守不住"本地优先"的承诺 —— 你的笔记将不得不放进一个由我们控制的浏览器沙盒，"本地"就成了营销话术。Skypad 唯一的网页部分，是你主动选择发布的页面。应用在 macOS、Windows、Linux、iOS、Android 上都是原生的。

05 · Security & privacy安全与隐私

What we can't
read, can't leak. 读不到的，
就泄不出。

Our security model is designed so the most interesting answer is boring: we hold ciphertext and nothing else. Five answers, each checkable against the audit reports. 我们的安全模型刻意把最有趣的答案做得无聊：我们手里只有密文，别无其他。五条回答，每一条都能对照审计报告核查。

01

What can Skypad servers actually read?Skypad 的服务器到底能读到什么？

Three things: how big an encrypted payload is, when it synced, and how many devices you have. Not the content, not the titles, not even the filenames — all of it is encrypted on your device before transmission, as in Fig. 1 above. If a court ordered us to hand over your notes, we'd hand over ciphertext we cannot open. 三样：一个加密数据包有多大、什么时候同步的、你有几台设备。读不到内容，读不到标题，连文件名也读不到 —— 这一切都在传输之前、在你的设备上加密完成，正如上方图 1。如果法院命令我们交出你的笔记，我们能交出的只有自己也打不开的密文。

02

What if I lose my passphrase?口令丢了怎么办？

We can't reset it — that's the price of a server that can't read your notes. At setup, Skypad asks you to print a recovery kit: 24 words that can re-derive your key. With the kit, full recovery. Without it, the server copy is gone for good, though notes already on your devices stay readable there. Print the kit. 我们无法重置 —— 这就是"服务器读不到你的笔记"的代价。初始设置时，Skypad 会请你打印一份恢复包：24 个单词，可以重新推导出你的密钥。有恢复包，就能完整恢复；没有它，服务器上那份就永远丢了，不过已经在你设备上的笔记仍然可读。请把恢复包打印出来。

03

Who checks these claims?这些说法谁来验证？

An independent Berlin security firm audits the sync protocol and the client every six months, and we publish each report in full — findings, severity, and our fixes, not a summary. The sync client's source is public, so the audit is repeatable by anyone. The next report is due July 2026. 一家独立的柏林安全公司每六个月审计一次同步协议和客户端，报告我们全文公开 —— 包括发现的问题、严重级别和我们的修复，不是只发摘要。同步客户端源码公开，任何人都可以复核审计。下一份报告在 2026 年 7 月发布。

04

What do you log, and for how long?你们记录什么日志，保留多久？

Account email, billing events, and sync timestamps — kept 30 days, then deleted. The apps contain no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK that phones home by default, and the website you're reading sets zero cookies. We can't sell what we don't collect, and we like it that way. 账号邮箱、计费事件、同步时间戳 —— 保留 30 天后删除。应用里没有数据分析 SDK，没有默认回传的崩溃上报 SDK，你正在读的这个网站一个 cookie 都不设。没收集的东西卖不出去，我们正喜欢这样。

05

Where do my encrypted notes live? GDPR?加密后的笔记存在哪里？符合 GDPR 吗？

On servers in Frankfurt, Germany, inside the EU, with encrypted backups in Amsterdam. A signed DPA is available to any customer on request at legal@skypad.page. Delete your account and every byte of your ciphertext is purged within 14 days — you get a confirmation email when the purge completes. 在德国法兰克福的服务器上，欧盟境内，加密备份在阿姆斯特丹。任何客户都可以写信到 legal@skypad.page 索取签署版 DPA。删除账号后，你的每一字节密文都会在 14 天内清除 —— 清除完成时你会收到一封确认邮件。

01 · The rhythm节奏

Small releases, often. 小步发布，勤快发布。

A minor release roughly every three weeks, a patch whenever a fix can't wait. No release trains held for marketing moments. 大约每三周一个次版本；修复等不了就出补丁。不为营销节点压着版本不发。

03 · Versioning版本号

Three numbers,
one promise. 三个数字，
一个承诺。

The middle number is the only one that usually moves: features land there, roughly every three weeks, and nothing breaks. Patches carry fixes only, shipped within the week. The first number is reserved for note-format changes — it hasn't moved since v3.0 in 2021. 平时只有中间那个数字在动：功能在这里落地，大约三周一次，什么都不会坏。补丁只带修复，当周就发。第一个数字留给笔记格式的变更——自 2021 年 v3.0 起，它再没动过。

When we retire a behaviour, the old way keeps working for two more minor releases, and the entry above says so in plain words. 要淘汰某个行为时，旧行为还会继续工作两个次版本，而且上面的记录会用白话写明这件事。

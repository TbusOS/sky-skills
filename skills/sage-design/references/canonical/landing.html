02 · What we chose我们的选择

Three decisions,
not compromising. 三个不让步
的决定。

The features most note apps advertise are exactly the ones we declined to build. Here's what we chose instead. 其他笔记软件拿来宣传的功能,恰恰是我们拒绝做的。下面是我们选择做的。

Your notes are plain Markdown.你的笔记就是 Markdown。

Every note is a .md file on your own disk. No proprietary format, no database, no lock-in. Open one in any editor — they're just text. 每条笔记都是你磁盘上的一个 .md 文件。没有私有格式,没有数据库,没有绑定。用任何编辑器打开 —— 就是纯文本。

How local-first works本地优先怎么工作

An editor that doesn't finish your sentence.不替你写完句子的编辑器。

We don't ship AI autocomplete, rewriting, or summaries. Your ideas are yours, formed in your own head, in your own words. Use AI somewhere else, on purpose. 我们不做 AI 自动补全、改写或摘要。你的想法是你的,在你脑子里、用你的话形成。要用 AI,请特意去别处用。

Why we said no我们为什么说不

We can't read your notes. By design.我们读不到你的笔记。这是有意的。

Your notes are encrypted on your device before sync. Our servers only ever see ciphertext. We publish an open-source security audit every six months. 笔记在你设备上加密后才同步。我们服务器只能看到密文。每 6 个月发布一次开源安全审计。

04 · Uses用法

Made for the way you think. 为你思考的方式而做。

Daily writing日常写作

Open to a blank page in under a second. Essays, field notes, letters to yourself. Export to PDF when ready. 一秒内打开空白页。文章、田野笔记、写给自己的信。写好了再导出 PDF。

Backlinks & graph反向链接与关系图

Link notes with [[wiki]] refs. Every backlink is visible. Grow a graph of your thinking. 用 [[wiki]] 风格链接笔记。每条反向链接都可见。慢慢生长出思考的关系图。

Sync on 5 devices5 台设备同步

Start on your laptop, finish on your phone. End-to-end encrypted — only ciphertext leaves your machine. 电脑上开始,手机上写完。端到端加密 —— 离开你设备的只有密文。

Publish one click一键发布

Any note becomes a public page at name.skypad.page. Custom domains on Team. No build step. 任意笔记一键变为 name.skypad.page 上的公开页。Team 支持自定义域名。无需构建。
